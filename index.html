Rekit : Record kit from REEF

When global data servers failed in 2105, nearly all recorded human history was lost. In response, REEF developed the RE KIT, a universal analog kit, to help individuals reclaim the act of writing, sensing, and remembering. Made from recycled marine plastic, it embodies the idea of reconstruction through residue.

RE-SOUND Pad

A modular pad that records through perforation. When inserted into an analog sound device, each hole pattern produces distinct tones - turning handwriting into memory through sound.

MONOLog

Protected by handwriting recognition and pressure sensors, MONOLog can be accessed only by you. Write freely - your words will never belong to anyone else.

Fade Letter

Each message fades away with time, allowing your words to breathe, vanish, and begin anew. Write, erase, and recirculate - transform memory into an eternal cycle of renewal.
